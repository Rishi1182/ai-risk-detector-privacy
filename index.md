# Privacy Policy – AI Risk Detector

**Last updated: 27 January 2026**

AI Risk Detector is a browser extension that helps users identify AI-generated content, phishing attempts, and coordinated fraud patterns.

---

## Data Collection

AI Risk Detector does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, passwords, payment details, or precise location data.

The extension processes only website content (such as visible text and links) that the user explicitly selects or initiates for analysis.

---

## Data Usage

Selected content is transmitted to a remote analysis service solely to perform risk classification and return results to the user.

The data is not used for advertising, tracking, profiling, or marketing purposes.

---

## Data Storage

Analysis results may be stored locally on the user’s device to allow users to review recent scans and compare writing styles.

No data is sold or shared with third parties.

---

## Remote Services

The extension communicates with a backend API for analysis purposes only.

No executable code is loaded remotely into the browser.

---

## User Control

All analysis actions are initiated by the user.

The extension does not automatically monitor browsing activity.
